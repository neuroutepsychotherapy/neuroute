About

Matthew Kennedy,
M.A., Registered Psychotherapist

Matthew (Matt) Kennedy brings over 15 years of experience in the mental health and addictions field. His practice is grounded in a neuroscience-based, nervous system-informed framework, recognizing the role of physiological state in emotional and psychological functioning. Drawing on principles of neuropsychotherapy, he integrates bottom-up and top-down approaches to support durable change.

Matthew is trained in Accelerated Resolution Therapy® (ART), a structured and effective approach for treating trauma, depression, anxiety, and other mental health concerns. ART uses guided eye movements to support the processing of distressing experiences, often without requiring clients to verbally relive or extensively describe them.

As one of the first clinicians in Canada trained in Tholáge® Neuropsychotherapy, Matthew works with suppressed or avoided emotional experiences, using guided techniques to help bring these experiences into awareness so they can be processed differently. Tholáge® is particularly effective for clients who feel stuck, disconnected, or unable to access underlying emotional responses through cognitive approaches alone.

Matthew is also a Certified HeartMath® Trauma-Sensitive Practitioner, offering HeartMath® coherence training and HRV biofeedback to help clients improve emotional regulation and build resilience through heart rate variability and physiological coherence practices.

Matthew incorporates vagus nerve stimulation (VNS) as part of a nervous system-informed approach. VNS may support shifts in physiological state and can be used to help clients move out of states such as shutdown or heightened stress. Matthew is among the first psychotherapists in Ontario bringing Dolphin Neurostim vagus nerve stimulation using microcurrent into clinical practice, offering this as part of in-person sessions to support nervous system regulation.

Known for his warmth, sense of humour, and signature dad jokes, Matthew brings levity into the therapeutic space, because tough work and laughter aren't mutually exclusive.

The NeuRoute™ Framework

Reset. Regulate.
Rewire. Repattern.

NeuRoute™ is an applied neuropsychotherapy framework targeting the nervous system as the foundation for emotional regulation, behavioural change, and durable recovery. It integrates state-based regulation with trauma memory reconsolidation, creating new neural routes of safety, agency, and flexibility.

01
Reset

Interrupt acute and chronic physiological stress states to create immediate access to calm, safety, and presence.

02
Regulate

Build autonomic flexibility and the ability to intentionally shift state, and reliably recover from activation.

03
Rewire

Create durable neuroplastic change in trauma and emotional memory networks at both event-specific and pattern-based levels.

04
Repattern

Consolidate new nervous system routes into identity, behaviour, and relationships so change becomes automatic and durable.
